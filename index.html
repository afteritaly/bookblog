Palazzo dei Diamanti is a key monument in Ferrara and a fundamental example of Renaissance architecture. The monument is part of the Addizione Erculea an urban expansion project carried out in Ferrara at the end of the 15th century, initiated by Duke Ercole I d’Este, during the rule of the Este court. This expansion secured a place for Ferrara in the UNESCO World Heritage List. Palazzo dei Diamanti was commissioned by Sigismondo d’Este and served as the residence of several members of the Este court family. Today, the Palazzo dei Diamanti functions as an important cultural institution, hosting the Pinacoteca Nazionale and major temporary exhibitions, yet it remains underrepresented in many structured digital resources. This made it an ideal subject for knowledge graph enrichment through semantic technologies, especially considering that one of our team members is originally from Ferrara.



Objectives

  • To fill the semantic gaps we were able to detect within ArCo
  • To better our abilities in the use of Large Language Models (LLMs), particularly ChatGPT and DeepSeek, in order to create RDF triples that would add meaningful and semantically accurate information to the ArCo knowledge graph
